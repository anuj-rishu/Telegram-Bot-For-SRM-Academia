SRM Academia Bot - Privacy Policy

Introduction

This Privacy Policy governs the manner in which SRM Academia Bot collects, uses, maintains, and discloses information collected from users of our Telegram bot service. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling any personal information we obtain from you with care and respect.

Information Collection

Personal Identification Information

We collect personal identification information from users in various ways, including:

  • Telegram User ID - Used to identify individual users and personalize services
  • Session Tokens - Temporary authentication tokens used to maintain your logged-in status

Academic Data

We may collect and store the following academic information:

  • Attendance Records - Your class attendance percentages and history
  • Academic Marks - Results from assessments, exams, and other evaluations
  • Course Information - Details about enrolled courses and academic schedule

How We Use Collected Information

SRM Academia Bot may collect and use your information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and improve our services
  • To personalize user experience
  • To send periodic alerts about attendance thresholds and academic updates
  • To process queries about academic information
  • To maintain session continuity without requiring frequent re-authentication

Data Security

We implement appropriate data collection, storage, and processing practices and security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your personal information and academic data.

  • All session tokens are encrypted using industry-standard encryption methods
  • Data is stored in secure server environments protected by firewalls
  • Regular security audits are conducted to ensure data protection
  • Access to user data is restricted to authorized personnel only

Data Retention

We will retain your personal information and academic data only for as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy. User data is retained as long as you actively use the bot and for a period not exceeding 6 months after inactivity.

Third-Party Services

We do not sell, trade, or rent users' personal identification information to others. We may use third-party service providers to help us operate our service, but these providers do not have access to personally identifiable information.

User Rights

As a user of SRM Academia Bot, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Object to processing of your personal data

Changes to This Privacy Policy

SRM Academia Bot reserves the right to update this privacy policy at any time. We will notify users of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on our website and through a notification in the bot. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
